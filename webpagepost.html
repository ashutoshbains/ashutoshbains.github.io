PriceWatch

a pricing analysis application

‘Price Watch’ is a real-time price monitoring and analysis application. Price Watch is designed with the purpose of helping a user monitor and keep track of all its competitors’ product prices as listed on their website, enabling the user to take data informed decisions related to their own product pricing strategies. While there are various types of industries and products that each company has to offer, Price Watch is specifically designed to cater to the needs of the businesses which offer a large number of products falling in various categories and subcategories.

The application's architecture is designed to be flexible and scalable, accommodating a wide range of industries and product categories. It incorporates data cleaning and validation processes to ensure data accuracy and reliability. A user-friendly interface complements the analytical capabilities, making it accessible to users with varying levels of technical expertise.

In this sense, Price Watch collects the data of all the products in each category and subcategory, and provides a subcategory-wide analysis for each store.
